Creation

How cars are created

Step 1 - Design

The first stage in car manufacturing is the design phase. During this step, engineers and designers work together to create detailed plans and digital models of the vehicle. They consider factors such as aerodynamics, safety, aesthetics, and functionality, using advanced software to visualize every component before any physical work begins.

Step 2 - Assembly

Once the design is finalized, the assembly phase begins. In this stage, various parts and systems—such as the engine, chassis, and interior—are manufactured and brought together on an assembly line. Skilled workers and robotic machines collaborate to efficiently assemble thousands of components, ensuring each car meets precise specifications.

Step 3 - Test

The final stage is testing. Each vehicle undergoes rigorous inspections and quality checks to ensure it meets safety and performance standards. This includes road tests, crash simulations, and detailed inspections of all systems. Only after passing these tests is the car approved for sale to customers.
